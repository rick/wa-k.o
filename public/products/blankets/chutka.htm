chutkas
sitemap: products > chutkas

The Chutka is a traditional textile of the Shauka tribe, living in the Central Himalayan region. It is a woolen pile blanket, which is usually made of two or more panels stitched together. The Chutka rugs at AVANI are created with the same technique with beautiful textures and lively colours.
