sitemap: products > pure silk shawls, stoles & mufflers

The pure silk products made by us are blends of four different kinds of wild silks: muga, eri, tussar and oak tussar. All these yarns are hand spun. Only very fine muga yarn is reeled yarn that is naturally gold in color. Eri silk is cultivated on the castor plant and oak tussar on oak trees in the Himalayas. Muga is now produced on indigenous fodder trees in the Kumaon hills called Litchia polyantha and Michelis bombacina. All the pure silk products are carrying SMOI certification by Silk Mark Of India, affliated to Central Silk Board, Ministry of Textiles, Government of India.
