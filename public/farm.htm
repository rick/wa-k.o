farm based activities
sitemap: farm based activities

Hill agriculture does not provide food security for the entire year and the need for cash forces the farmers or members of their families to migrate to urban centers. In order to facilitate a postive shift in the overall land use, we have initiated a program on income generation through land based activities. A few activities have been started with the farmers and groups, and the program will develop as more experience is gained in increasing the income generating potential of these activities.
